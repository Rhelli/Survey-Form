Scurvy Form

Avast ye, some o' ye 'ave been complainin' that a plight o' scurvy has been washin' around th' deck. Fill out ye form 'n hand o'er ye information so that we may defeat this bilge-sucklin' scourge that be threatenin' t' sink every last one o' us ole salt. Do nah be a scurvy dog, lest ye walk th' plank t' become taint'd shark bait!
